The application process
1
Application (you are here)
Complete this form with your personal details, employment information and current living situation. The landlord will use this to assess your suitability and affordability.
2
Referencing
If your application is shortlisted, a formal reference check will be carried out by Vorensys, an independent referencing provider, covering your credit history, employment and previous landlord.
3
Tenancy Agreement
Once referencing is complete, a tenancy agreement will be prepared for you to review and sign digitally.
4
Inventory & Move-in
Before moving in, a property inventory will be completed and shared with you digitally so both parties have an agreed record of the property's condition.
📋 Data sharing notice
If your application is shortlisted, your name, contact details and rental information will be shared with Vorensys Ltd to carry out a formal reference check. You will be contacted before this occurs and may withdraw your application at any time.
