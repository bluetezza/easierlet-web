Privacy notice

Last updated 17 April 2026

This notice explains how Oakrise Estates Ltd ("we", "us"), trading as easierLet, handles the personal information you provide when applying for a rental property through this service. It's written in plain English — the short version is: we only use your information for the rental application you've started, we don't sell it, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

Who's the data controller?

For applications to properties owned by Oakrise Estates Ltd, we are the data controller. For applications to properties owned by other landlords using easierLet, the landlord is the data controller and we act as their data processor — we provide the tools but they decide what's done with your information.

What we collect

When you submit a pre-application, we collect:

  • Contact details — name, email, phone, date of birth, nationality
  • Household details — who's moving in, pets, smoking, guarantor availability
  • Right to Rent information — your status in the UK (we don't need to see documents at this stage)
  • Employment and income — employer, job title, gross income
  • Self-declared history — any adverse credit, evictions, or rent arrears
  • Tenancy preferences — move-in date, length, reason for moving

Why we collect it

To let the landlord decide whether to shortlist your application and invite you to the next stage (formal referencing). Without this information, we can't process a rental application — it's the minimum needed for the landlord to make an informed decision.

The legal basis is your consent (which you give by submitting the form) and legitimate interest (the landlord needs to assess suitability for tenancy).

Who sees it

Your application is seen by:

  • The landlord of the property you're applying to
  • Our platform (Supabase, based in the EU) to store the data securely
  • If you're shortlisted and consent to referencing: the referencing provider the landlord uses

We don't share your application with other landlords. We don't sell your data. We don't use it for advertising.

How long we keep it

If your application is unsuccessful, we keep it for up to 12 months in case you apply for another property and want to reuse the data. If it's successful and you move in, we keep it for the duration of the tenancy plus 7 years, as required for tax and regulatory purposes.

You can request deletion at any time by emailing the address below — we'll remove what we can, keeping only what we're legally required to retain.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct anything that's wrong
  • Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention)
  • Withdraw consent for future processing
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk

Security

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Access is limited to the landlord handling your application and their authorised staff. We use reputable, ISO-27001-certified infrastructure providers.

Cookies

This site uses a small number of essential cookies needed for the application form to work. We don't use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Contact

For any data protection question, email privacy@easierlet.com. We aim to respond within 5 working days.
