3. What the service does

easierLet provides software to help UK landlords manage tenancies — including but not limited to:

  • Property and document management (certificates, agreements, inventories)
  • Tenant onboarding, referencing and pre-application screening
  • Tenancy agreement generation and digital signing
  • Maintenance request triage and scheduling
  • Property visit notice and confirmation flow
  • Income / expense recording and per-property reporting

We may add, change or remove features over time. We'll let you know in advance about removals that materially affect how you use the service.

What we don't do. easierLet is a tool, not a regulated service. We don't:

  • Provide legal advice. Tenancy agreements generated by the platform are based on a standard Assured Periodic Tenancy template; you should review them yourself or with a solicitor.
  • Hold or protect deposits ourselves. You must protect deposits in an approved scheme (DPS, mydeposits, TDS) within 30 days.
  • Act as a letting agent or guarantor.
  • Carry out Right-to-Rent checks for you. The app records your declarations but the legal obligation remains with you.
